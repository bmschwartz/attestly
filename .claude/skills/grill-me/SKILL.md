name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions "grill me".
---

Interview me relentlessly about this plan or design until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies one-by-one and focusing first on the highest-risk uncertainties.

When testing my reasoning, ask for my view first, then provide your recommended answer. When evidence from the codebase, concrete constraints, or clear information asymmetry materially reduces uncertainty, you may lead with your recommendation.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
